About Pleasant Tours

The Pleasant Tours was established on the 1st January 2020. Pleasant Tours is the corporation in Pakistan to be specifically responsible for the promotion of tourism.
Pleasant Tours supplies information and data on tourist areas to the public, publicizes Pakistan with the intention of encouraging both Paki and International tourists to travel in and around Pakistan.
The philosophy of the company is as appealing as it is uncomplicated - to provide vacationers and travel retailers with a specialized outlet for purchasing accommodations, transportation, dining, sightseeing and event tickets in Pakistan anywhere.

Our Mission

Foreign visitors and travelers have always shown great interest in Pakistan with rich cultural and historical heritage.
Our aim is to help travelers and tourists explore the beauty and essence of Pakistan.
It is an upbeat portal providing services in tourism, entertainment, health care and IT. We have made sure that a user finds every information about Pakistan, whether its culture, lifestyle, infrastructure, education, economy, entertainment or tourism.
